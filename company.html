 Our Company

A team of professionals and experienced limousine drivers at Al Thybeen Limousine


Al Thybeen Limousine is a prestige car hire company in Qatar. The company has had tremendous exposure to international business environment following the boom of Doha as a global interest after Dubai in the region. Qatar being a country with one of the world’s highest per capita income has boosted the company’s business which was incorporated as an entity renting and leasing prestige cars and light motor vehicles.

With a staggering experience of more than a decade and a half in the gulf region including Dubai, Abu Dhabi and Qatar, in finance, business development and management, Mr Harunoor Rashid established and successfully expanded the company over the years. With its headquarters in Al Khor, the company has operations all over Qatar. With a significant number of European client base in Qatar, the company has taken up a venture to enter into the UK market, which is currently experiencing massive growth in the industry. Al Thybeen will obtain brand new low emission petrol engine and electric vehicles from the manufacturers on lease and rent them to the Private Hire sector, i.e. minicab companies, Uber drivers, Ola drivers, etc. the current industry trend is that not a single minicab company or driver own a vehicle. They rent the vehicles from companies like Al Thybeen.

Al Thybeen intends to be profitable and continue to improve financial performance.
